The best day of my life occurred when I was 14 years old. There was a football video game at the time called Pro Evolution Soccer, made by Konami. And the latest version of that game had recently come out and I decided that I was going to get it that day, but I just needed everything to go perfectly on that day. So I got up, went to school. I didn't get an end-chobble at school. It was normal for me to get in trouble and to have to go to detention. When I didn't get an end-chobble that day, on the way home from school, I stopped off at my local video game store, bought the game, went home and played it into the evening. And from that day until this day, it's always stayed in my mind as such a perfect idyllic day, which I wouldn't change.
