My favorite place to visit is probably Japan, specifically Kyoto. The flight there, however, was really, really horrible. It's a 12-hour flight. After I get off, I feel very gross and just jet lagged, but it's all worth it because in the end I get to see and experience and try many different foods. I get to live for a few days in a completely different culture and experience a new language. My favorite food that I had there was probably the curry udon. It's something that you can find in America, but it's just special, I think, in Japan. They're using the freshest ingredients. The sushi there is really great as well. It's great to visit in the springtime when you can see the cherry blossoms bloom.
