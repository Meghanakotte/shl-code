My favorite place is visiting Hyderabad, a city in South Indian, South Indian has a rich history and is known for its cultural heritage, architecture and cuisines. One of the most iconic landmarks in Hyderabad is the Charminar, a 16th century Monument with four Grand Arches located in the heart of the Old City, the city also famous for its gold conda fort, a massive fort, a dress with impressive acoustics and panoramic views of the surrounding area. Another notable attraction is the Chowamalla Palai.
