Ah, the school playground. What a wonderful place. We had a lot of concrete. There wasn't any astro turf. In my day, we had a football pitch, a concrete one. We had goals that we made with our jumpers. We had teachers patrolling around the area and making sure people weren't doing what they weren't supposed to be doing, which they were in the corners of the playground. You know, around the sides, there was a lot of greenery, so people would hide behind it. Things would happen. And people would get caught. But everyone else would be playing football. They might be playing games. They might be playing with the girls. They might be playing with the boys, but all around.
