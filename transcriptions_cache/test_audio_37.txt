Imagine a vibrant market place in vanilla, bustling with activities, vendors with colorful stalls or shutting out with their wares, voiceless, lively, the air is filled with enticing aroma of spices, fresh produce and sizzling food. Should your eyes meet with excitement, you can navigate through the market and carefully examine everything from the hand-made crops to exotic delicacies that the merchants are currently selling to you. I know it is sensory overload but the market offers a lot of variety that you could visit.
